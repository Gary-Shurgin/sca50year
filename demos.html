50 Year Arts and Sciences Challenges
Several Artisans across the Known World are working hard at building 50 year challenge projects.  For some, it is an effort to make fifty of something, for others, it is a project specific to the 50 year event, and will forever become part of our history.  Some spectacular efforts include the "50 Years of history" Tapestry that highlights key events from our past.  Or two foot by four foot hand stitched tapestries with the kingdom arms of each of the 19 kingdoms. 
If you have a A&S 50 Challenge that you would like featured, feel free to contact the Arts and Sciences Deputy for 50 year.  We can link to your website if you have one, or feature pictures of the project in progress. 
Learn more about ongoing projects on the Arts & Sciences Challenges page.
