Special Events
What is a Special Event?
A Special Event is one you would like on the calendar, for everyone to know about. Does your Kingdom want to sponsor a Known World Party? Have a Multi Kingdom Court with you and your closest Kingdoms? How about the old fashioned practice of ‘Squire Tossing’? Whatever it is, consider enlisting our local coordinators to help you find the materials you need and of course to get it on the main schedule of must see events for our Guests.

Blue Chip events are the best we have in historic or Society display. Have something that could be a Blue Chip event? See the description and current schedule on the Blue Chip Link in Schedules on the menu.

Coordinate with our Special Projects Deputy to make the most of your event!
