Schedule of Events
The Schedule is not available yet.
We can tell you there are plenty of activities around the clock. Most activities have dedicated buildings that can run through extended hours. Classes are being vetted now and we even expect to have a late night tavern and a separate late night dance hall.

We are planning on having "Blue Chip" events that feature the best the SCA has to offer. No Blue Chip event will conflict with another, and one should be able to go from one to the next and experience the very finest events. The Blue Chip schedule will be posted here as soon as it is available.

If you have questions or concerns, or a suggestion for an event, I'll be happy to pass it on to the correct party for consideration and scheduling. I'm here to help.

Sir William of Fairhaven, OP, BC (Dayton, OH)
Sir William is a 30 year Veteran of the Society, an accomplished musician scheduling tours around the world, and a consummate professional when it comes to scheduling and resolving conflicts. He has served the Society and Crown as advisor on international committees and has served his Kingdom as Earl Marshal.
